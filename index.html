FilmWhisper Reorder Addon

About

This Stremio addon enhances your FilmWhisper experience by automatically placing AI Recommendations at the top of your search results.

Features

  • Prioritizes AI-driven movie recommendations.
  • Prioritizes AI-driven series recommendations.
  • Keeps your top content front and center.

Installation

Click the button below to install the addon in Stremio:

Install in Stremio

How to Use

After installing the addon, simply search for movies or series within Stremio. The AI recommendations from FilmWhisper will automatically be moved to the top of the results.
